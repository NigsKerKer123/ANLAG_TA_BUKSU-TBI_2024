ANLAG TA BUKSU-TBI

    Anlag Ta Buksu Technology Business Incubator (TBI) is a premier initiative of Bukidnon State University (BUKSU), designed to foster technology-driven entrepreneurship and innovation in the region. In line with the university’s commitment to sustainable development and its strategic role as a leader in research, innovation, and technology commercialization, the TBI aims to nurture startups from concept to market-ready businesses.

Learn more

Vision

A premier institution that fosters innovative and ethical leaders for sustainable development, driving growth and technological advancement in Bukidnon and beyond.

Mission

To empower and support entrepreneurs in the development of technology based startups, accelerating their growth from idea to market success while contributing to regional economic development through innovation, education, and collaboration.

Goal

To contribute to local, regional and national economic development by advancing the entrepreneurial culture in the university, nurturing the growth of new technology- based startups, and accelerating the development of young entrepreneurial units from 'idea stage' to independent self-sustaining successful business in the region.

More about ANLAG TA BUKSU-TBI

  • The Anlag Ta Buksu Technology Business Incubator (TBI) is a unique university-based incubator in Mindanao that supports new ventures by providing essential infrastructure, resources, and business guidance. It offers office and laboratory spaces with advanced technology, access to specialized equipment for prototyping and testing, and mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs and industry professionals. Additionally, it connects startups with investors, funding agencies, and government resources to foster growth. More than just a workspace, the TBI serves as a collaborative ecosystem, linking entrepreneurs with academia, government, and the private sector to promote innovation and development.
